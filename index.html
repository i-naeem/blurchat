Frequently Asked Questions

Is BlurChat free to use?

Yes, BlurChat is completely free to download and use.

Does BlurChat work on mobile devices?

Currently, BlurChat is designed as a Chrome extension for desktop use. We're exploring options for mobile support in the future.

Can I still use WhatsApp Web normally with BlurChat installed?

BlurChat only activates when you toggle it on. When off, WhatsApp Web functions normally.

Is my data safe with BlurChat?

BlurChat does not collect or store any of your personal data or messages. It simply applies a visual blur effect to your browser.
